Driving Trade and Investment Across Africa

The Trade and Investment Management Portal is Afreximbank’s digital gateway for facilitating trade, mobilizing investment, and connecting African opportunities with global capital. The portal provides a centralized platform where investors, businesses, governments, and financial institutions can discover investment opportunities, promote projects, and engage with strategic partners.

Designed to support Afreximbank’s mandate of promoting intra-African trade and economic development, the portal brings together key stakeholders across the investment ecosystem to accelerate deal-making, strengthen partnerships, and unlock Africa’s growth potential.

Through the portal, users gain access to curated investment opportunities, structured networking engagements, project promotion tools, and investor engagement platforms that enable efficient capital mobilization and investment facilitation across the continent.
