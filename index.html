A single place to coordinate opportunities and engagement

TIMP brings key workflows into one portal: opportunity intake, deal visibility, event profiling, and structured follow-up. Stakeholders can discover relevant opportunities, request materials, and engage through lightweight messaging — while Admin curates and governs what goes public.

In the full build, the same structure supports controlled access, audit-ready publishing, analytics, and integrations for scheduling, notifications, and document governance.
